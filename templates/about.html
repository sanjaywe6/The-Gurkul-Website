About The Gurkul

Founded to accelerate the nation's educational and industrial revolution. The Gurkul is profoundly Indian. With ingenuity and drive, our graduates have invented fundamental technologies, launched New branch and created lots of indian jobs. At the same time and without the slightest sense of contradiction , The Gurkul is profounly global. Our Community gains tremendous strength as a magner for talent from around the world, through teaching , reasearch and innovation. The Gurkul's exceptional Community pursues its mission of service to the nation and the world.

We seek to develop in each member of the Gurkul Community the ability and passion to work wisely, creative, and effective for the betterment of humankind.
