Gurkul Path

The Gurkul emphasizes on practical knowledge which is quite beneficial to build the concepts of the students.

Learning beyond classrooms through extra-curricular activities for all-round development.

Educating a person does not mean that he/she became a rich person. It means to do what is right and in the right way. That's what The Gurkul system teaches.

The Gurkul is a contraction of Sanskrit word guru means teacher and kula mean family or home. Students(shishya) live away from their family but they get a new family. Gurkul includes humanity, deep thinking, respect, moral living, the importance of duty, science, non-violence, values, ethics, knowledge of application are well appreciated.
